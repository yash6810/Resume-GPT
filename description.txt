ResumeGPT is an AI-powered resume analyzer and ATS booster built with FastAPI, spaCy, and sentence-transformers. Features: Resume parsing (PDF/DOCX), ATS scoring with 6 platform simulations (LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, Glassdoor, Monster, Lever), A/B testing with callback tracking, AI cover letter generator, Resume builder with 7 templates, Job application tracker, and Interview prep generator. A free & open source alternative to Jobscan ($50/mo), Teal ($29/mo), and Rezi ($29/mo).
